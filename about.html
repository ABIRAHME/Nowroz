Our Story

Nowroz was founded in 2018 with a simple mission: to create high-quality, sustainable t-shirts that look good, feel good, and do good for the planet.

Our journey began when our founder, Sarah, noticed the environmental impact of fast fashion while traveling through textile-producing regions. Determined to make a difference, she assembled a team of like-minded designers and sustainability experts to create a brand that prioritizes both style and environmental responsibility.

Today, Nowroz has grown into a community of conscious consumers who believe that fashion doesn't have to come at the expense of our planet. We continue to innovate and improve our practices to minimize our environmental footprint while maximizing comfort and style.

Our Mission

We're on a mission to transform the fashion industry by proving that sustainability and style can go hand in hand.

Sustainable Materials

We use only 100% organic cotton and recycled materials in our products, reducing water usage and eliminating harmful chemicals from the production process.

Ethical Production

We partner with factories that provide fair wages, safe working conditions, and opportunities for growth. We believe that ethical production is just as important as sustainable materials.

Community Impact

We donate 5% of our profits to environmental initiatives and community projects that align with our values. We believe in giving back to the planet and the communities that support us.
